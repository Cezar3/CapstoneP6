RECAP OF CASE STUDY - The Techniclinic at Oakland tech provides services that aim to remedy the effects of child abuse locally and with ease of access. The youth of today have been ravaged by the effects of evolving technology and the covid pandemic. The techniclinic even widens it's reach to "nearby Oakland International High School and recent graduates from both schools up to the age of 21" . They have resources that are versatile such as the choice between individual and group counseling, they even teach students how to spread their own messages with leadership and presentation skills being taught to increase communication as a whole within the community.
The MOST EFFECTIVE method for lessening the effects of child abuse is screening children for various signs of abuse early in life. The Techniclinic succeeds at this method by making resources to be screened various residuals of child abuse readily available within the community of Oakland Tech. These teenage years are when the effects of these abuses become much more apparent and for most it is one of the most confusing times of their lives. That fact makes it imperative for an organization like the techniclinic to exist for furthering the research and recieving data about how kids today are affected by the world around them.
USE OF TECHNOLOGY - The Techniclinic uses their instagram accounts to spread information regarding the services they provide in a way that is effective because it is accessible to the youth of today @laclinica_techniclinic as well as its shared posts with @othsconnections create a convenient and even enticing avenue for students of oakland tech to find resources regarding the perplexing topics of mental and sexual health. This account uses short-form content that displays vibrant colors and a warm community. They also have a highlight with popping red text that allows you to book an appointment from the convenience of an app like instagram.
IMPLICATIONS FOR: My project will be to compile all these realities of childhood trauma and abuse into a game that will assist the affected people in a way that feels less like they are test subjects and more like they are not alone in their dysfunctions
I may incorporate many factors that make the techniclinic so thorough into my game and create something that can resonate at the level of something so thoughtfully built such as the techniclinic.
